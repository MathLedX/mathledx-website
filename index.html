About Rich

MathLedX is personal because the work is personal.

Rich Hollinger teaches math at San Marino High School and built MathLedX out of a frustration with how often capable students get misread. They are called careless, unmotivated, or not math people when the real problem is usually more specific and more fixable than that.

The project brings together classroom teaching, published ideas, future video lessons, and tutoring under one name so families can see the whole philosophy, not just the booking link.
