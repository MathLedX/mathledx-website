About Rich

Hi, I'm Rich.

I'm a high school math teacher and math specialist. I help students rebuild confidence by making math make sense step by step.

I know what it feels like to look capable on the outside and still not have a real system underneath. I coasted for a long time, hit the wall hard, and had to learn how I actually think before math started to click in a durable way.

MathLedX comes out of that process. It is the combination of classroom teaching, written ideas, visual explanation, and one-on-one support that I wish more students had access to earlier.

The goal is not to make math look flashy. The goal is to make it clear, honest, and usable so students can stop guessing and start understanding.
