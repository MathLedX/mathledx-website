What is it?

Practice math by battling monsters

Math Monsters turns systematic math practice into an adventure. Each monster represents a math concept. Solve problems correctly to defeat them — get it wrong, and the monster fights back.

🧮
Engagement

Game-based learning

Students are motivated by defeating monsters, leveling up, and unlocking new math worlds — not just drilling worksheets.

📈
Progression

Systematic levels

Every level is mapped to a real curriculum concept. Students can't skip ahead until they've mastered the foundation underneath.

⚡
Feedback

Instant explanations

When a student gets a problem wrong, the monster explains exactly where the thinking went wrong — so the learning happens in real time.

The Monsters

Meet your opponents

Each monster guards a different branch of math. Master the concept to defeat them and move forward.

➕
Count Addus

Guardian of arithmetic. Defeat him with addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

🔺
The Fraction Phantom

A ghost that multiplies into fractions. You must simplify your way through to survive.

📐
Algebratron

A robot who speaks only in variables. Solve for x — or face the consequences.

🌀
The Derivative Drake

A dragon who regenerates unless you find the rate of change. Calculus is your only weapon.

📊
Statistica

She hides in probability distributions. Your understanding of statistics is the only flashlight.

🧊
Geometry Golem

Made of shapes and angles. Prove your theorems or be frozen in an unsolved proof.
